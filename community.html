Umzamomhle Community Garden

Situated at Dibinkonzo Village, Sterkspruit, Eastern Cape

Umzamomhle Community Garden was registered with Department of Social Development as a Non Profit Organization (NPO number: 162-995 NPO) in December 2015. It was established in 2012 at Dibinkonzo Village, Sterkspruit by 16 community members consisting of women, men and youth. The objective of the project is to alleviate poverty, hunger, crime and unemployment. It is an agricultural project that focuses on vegetable production for consumption and selling.

Types of vegetables produced:
  • Pumpkin
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Green Beans
  • Beetroot
  • Gem Squash
  • Butternut
  • Cabbage
  • Peas
  • Lettuce
  • Green Pepper
